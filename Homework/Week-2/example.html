Tourist attractions in Denmark:

  • About Nyhavn:

    The colourful houses of Nyhavn are one of the most iconic spots in Copenhagen. Eat and drink by the canal in the sun or take a boat tour and explore.

  • About møns klint:

    Møns Klint is a 6 km stretch of limestone and chalk cliffs along the eastern coast of the Danish island of Møn in the Baltic Sea. Some of the cliffs fall a sheer 120 m to the sea below.

  • About Tivoli:

    The park is best known for its wooden roller coaster, Rutschebanen, or as some people call it, Bjergbanen (The Mountain Coaster), built in 1914. It is one of the world's oldest wooden roller coasters that is still operating today.
